Privacy Policy for Video Browser App

Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Browser App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • We do not store user credentials or login information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not store or cache video content

2.2 Information We May Collect

  • Search queries (temporarily, not stored persistently)
  • App usage analytics (anonymous, via Google Play Console)
  • Crash reports (anonymous, via Google Play Console)

3. YouTube Data API Usage

Our app uses the YouTube Data API to:

  • Search for and display video information
  • Show video thumbnails and metadata
  • Enable video playback through YouTube's embedded player

Important: We do not download, store, or cache any video content. All video playback is handled directly by YouTube.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except:

  • YouTube/Google: Search queries are sent to YouTube Data API to fetch video information
  • Google Play Services: Anonymous usage analytics and crash reports

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. Our app uses secure HTTPS connections for all API communications.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app implements content filtering to provide age-appropriate content. However, we recommend parental supervision for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • YouTube Data API: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • YouTube Player: Subject to YouTube's Terms of Service
  • Google Play Services: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
